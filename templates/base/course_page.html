Courses
About the Courses
The courses take 3-full days and are aimed at small groups of up to 7 people. Our courses are ideal for young Ph.D. candidates and postdocs who want to gain control of their own experiments. We are strongly committed to the idea that software should enhance the capabilities of hardware and not the other way around.

Target audience

We don't require specific programming knowledge to follow the course. If you have a bit of background with Python, Matlab or any other language, understand what an if-statement is, and how a for-loop works, then you are ready to follow the workshops.
